Before we continue...

If you want to elaborate our product you'll need: two glass bottles, a piece of cardboard, aluminum foil, and a piece of cloth

Learn More

Share:

Methodology

Step 1

Pour the dirty water in one of the bottles and put each end of the piece of cloth in each one
Step 2

Wait for the water to pass from one bottle to another, then wrap the piece of cardboard with the aluminum foil.
Step 3

Put the bottle with clean water in front of the solar panel and wait 35 minutes, when you finish all of the steps ¡You are done!

About our product

  • Objective

    Our objective is to facilitate a simple and cost-effective method for purifying contaminated water, and improving acces to clean and safe drinking water in regions facing water quality issues.

  • Introduction

    Filtration is used in various processes such as purification, separation, clarification, and product recovery. Nowadays, 771 million people lack acces to clean water, that's 1 in 10 people on the planet, with our product we are trying to help people little by little.

  • Conclusion

    This innovate water purification system will transform contaminated water into a cleaner and safer resource in an affordable way.
